Nuwara Eliya – Little England

Nuwara Eliya – Little England

Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as “Little England,” is a picturesque hill station nestled in the central highlands of Sri Lanka. With its cool climate, lush greenery, and colonial charm, it offers a distinct and refreshing experience for visitors.

Tour details
Kandy

Temple of the Tooth Relic

Temple of the Tooth Relic

The Temple of the Tooth, also known as Sri Dalada Maligawa, is a sacred Buddhist temple located in the city of Kandy, Sri Lanka. It houses the relic of the tooth of Lord Buddha, which is considered one of the most sacred objects in Buddhism.
