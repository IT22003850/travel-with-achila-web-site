Hot Air Ballooning

As the multi-coloured hot-air balloon drifts silently above the forest, adventure takes on a whole new meaning.

Hot air ballooning in Sri Lanka is a popular activity that allows you to experience the country’s beauty in a unique and enchanting way. As you ascend into the sky in a colorful balloon, you’ll be mesmerized by the stunning landscapes unfolding below.

The hill country is a favorite destination for hot air ballooning in Sri Lanka. Imagine floating above the emerald-green tea plantations of Nuwara Eliya or Ella, with misty mountains as your backdrop. The panoramic views of the rolling hills, terraced fields, and cascading waterfalls create a truly magical experience.

For a historical and cultural adventure, consider a hot air balloon ride over Sigiriya. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is famous for its ancient rock fortress perched atop a massive rock formation. Soaring above Sigiriya, you’ll have a bird’s-eye view of the intricate gardens, moats, and the iconic Lion Rock. The sunrise or sunset balloon rides offer particularly breathtaking vistas.

If you prefer coastal landscapes, hot air ballooning in coastal areas like Bentota or Galle provides a different perspective. Drifting over the turquoise waters and golden beaches, you’ll witness the beauty of Sri Lanka’s coastline from a serene and elevated vantage point. Keep an eye out for marine life, such as dolphins or sea turtles, swimming below.

Hot air balloon rides in Sri Lanka are typically conducted in the early morning or late afternoon when the winds are calm and the lighting is ideal for capturing stunning photographs. The duration of the rides can vary, ranging from one to two hours, depending on weather conditions and the specific itinerary.

Safety is a top priority for hot air balloon operators in Sri Lanka, and experienced pilots ensure a smooth and enjoyable journey. It’s important to follow the instructions provided by the crew and dress comfortably, considering the altitude and weather conditions.

Whether you’re a thrill-seeker, a photography enthusiast, or simply looking for a unique way to appreciate Sri Lanka’s beauty, hot air ballooning offers an unforgettable adventure. It’s an opportunity to create cherished memories and witness the breathtaking landscapes of this incredible island nation from a whole new perspective.
